### Scorecard Against All Feedback Rules

#### PASSED
- [ ] **No "Not X, but Y"** — Scanned entire chapter. Any instances caught and replaced?
- [ ] **No character-as-author** — Does any character articulate the theme cleanly? If so, delete.
- [ ] **No therapy-speak** — "Processing," "boundaries," "holding space," "clear-eyed acknowledgment" — all absent?
- [ ] **No bold on thematic lines** — Zero bold? Position earns weight, not formatting.
- [ ] **Register degradation** — If characters are physically exhausted, is the prose plain?
- [ ] **Failed articulation** — At least one moment where a character can't finish a thought?
- [ ] **Failed comfort** — At least one consolation attempt that doesn't land?
- [ ] **Dialogue misalignment** — At least one emotional exchange where characters talk past each other?
- [ ] **Era-appropriate psychology** — No modern concepts in historical/fantasy settings?
- [ ] **Attraction awkwardness** — If romantic arc, are there genuinely awkward beats before resolution?
- [ ] **Antagonist weight** — Do antagonists get proportional screen time and interiority?
- [ ] **Shrinking focus** — When macro-stakes peak, does the protagonist fixate on a micro-detail?
- [ ] **Competence tax** — Does hyper-competence cause at least one failure or misinterpretation?
- [ ] **Unresolved loop guard** — Is the central unanswered question still actively uncomfortable?
- [ ] **Anchor distribution** — Are sensory anchors fired at appropriate intervals?

### Chapter-Specific Checks

**Act I chapters only:**
- [ ] Opening hook contains unresolved element
- [ ] Master anchor planted in first paragraph
- [ ] Central unresolved loop seeded
- [ ] One failed comfort / failed connection moment

**Act II chapters only:**
- [ ] At least one plan backfires (genuine failure, not near-miss)
- [ ] Confusion technique deployed
- [ ] Breath Beat (every 3rd chapter): failed articulation, ugly physical detail, dialogue shorter than satisfying

**Act III chapters only:**
- [ ] Identity-level insight delivered
- [ ] Insight costs something — a belief is lost, not just transformed
- [ ] Shrinking focus: micro-detail at peak macro-stakes

**Act IV chapters only:**
- [ ] One relationship beat unresolved or awkward
- [ ] Loop closure: all high-urgency loops resolved except the intentional open loop
- [ ] Post-hypnotic trigger delivered explicitly
- [ ] Tidy Ending Test: at least one arc (guilt/romance/family/politics) has a gap
