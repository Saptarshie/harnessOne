## Anti-Patterns

- Don't include the RSO or loop status in the chapter file. That lives in `architecture/`.
- Don't number chapters with digits in the filename (`01-the-weight-of-a-seal.md` is the filename convention; the displayed number in the heading is spelled out).
- Don't add a "Previously on..." summary. The RSO serves that purpose for the Weaver; readers either remember or don't need one.
