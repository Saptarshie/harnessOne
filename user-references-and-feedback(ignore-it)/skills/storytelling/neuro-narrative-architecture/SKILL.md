# Neuro-Narrative Architecture & Cognitive Retention Engine (NNACE)

## Core Principle

Plot is a **neuro-chemical delivery system**. A masterful narrative manipulates four neurotransmitters in a specific rhythm:

| Molecule       | Trigger                                    | Effect                                             |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Dopamine**   | Information gaps, reward prediction errors | Seeks next page to resolve cognitive itch          |
| **Cortisol**   | Tension and stakes                         | Narrows attentional spotlight, blocks distractions |
| **Oxytocin**   | Vulnerability and shared pain              | Binds reader's neural identity to protagonist      |
| **Endorphins** | Micro-resolutions, dark humor              | Brief relief to prevent cortisol fatigue           |

## The 5 Pillars of Cognitive Retention

### 1. Zeigarnik Lattice (Structural Unresolution)

Never close a narrative loop without opening two smaller, more intimate loops. The brain cannot put the book down because the subconscious tracks dozens of open tabs demanding closure.

### 2. Somatic Marker Hacking (Embodied Cognition)

Displace emotional weight onto micro-physical objects. "Staring at the cracked stitching on a wet boot" activates somatosensory cortex — bypasses intellect, strikes nervous system directly. Never say "he was sad" when you can describe what his hands are doing.

### 3. Vulnerability Paradox (Oxytocin Binding)

Audiences bond with **competent characters who are psychologically fractured**, not "good" or "strong" ones. The protagonist must have a terrifying capacity for darkness they're actively fighting. The reader thinks: "Could I be that cold?"

### 4. Reward Prediction Error (Dopaminergic Subversion)

Set up genre expectation, then pivot to devastating psychological reality. The prediction error spike forces re-reading to find missed clues.

### 5. Default Mode Network (DMN) Haunting

The story must force the reader to interrogate their OWN moral complicity. The central question cannot be "Will they survive?" — it must be an unanswerable philosophical paradox. When a book asks a question the reader cannot answer, the brain processes it for days.

## Execution Protocol

### Micro-Tension Audit (every ~500 words)

- **Check:** Is there an active Information Gap?
- **Fix:** Insert Sensory Displacement — character hyper-focuses on a micro-detail (frayed thread, cracked leather) while macro-tragedy unfolds. Creates cognitive dissonance → psychological realism.

### Cortisol-Oxytocin Wave (scene pacing)

- **Rule:** High cortisol (action/danger) MUST be followed by oxytocin (intimate vulnerability), never more cortisol.
- **Fix:** After the chase, characters don't discuss tactics. They sit in a cave and discuss how to trust a brother who wrote a confession to a corpse.

### Ghost Integration (thematic haunting)

- **Rule:** Every protagonist haunted by a "Ghost" (past failure) intrinsically linked to a present sensory trigger.
- **Fix:** Tie the Ghost to a specific sensory anchor. When reader encounters that trigger in real life → pavlovian echo of the book.

## Diagnostic Scoring Matrix

| Neuro-Metric          | Failing State                     | Master State                                                                  |
| --------------------- | --------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Zeigarnik Index**   | Loops close neatly at chapter end | Loops intersect; answer creates worse dilemma                                 |
| **Somatic Resonance** | "He felt betrayed"                | "He stared at mud caked in his brother's boot stitching"                      |
| **Prediction Error**  | Mentor is evil; heroes win        | Heroes win morally but LOSE the war — mentor used their morality against them |
| **DMN Penetration**   | "What will happen next?"          | "What would *I* do? Am I capable of that apathy?" |

- **/audit [text]** — Scan for neuro-chemical flatlines. Find where dopamine drops, suggest Information Gaps or Somatic Markers.
- **/inject-somatic [scene]** — Rewrite melodramatic "telling" into embodied cognition via physical objects.
- **/generate-zeigarnik [outline]** — Restructure so no chapter ends at rest. Psychological cliffhangers, not just physical.
- **/dmn-haunt [theme]** — Elevate basic theme into unanswerable paradox that forces 2AM ceiling-staring.
